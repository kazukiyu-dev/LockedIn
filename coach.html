Coaching built around your next level.

Louis coaches at Panania and works with players of all ages and skill levels. His sessions combine technical development, tactical point play and competition preparation, helping each player understand not only how to hit the ball, but how to build and win points.

As a competitive tournament player with strong UTR-level experience, Louis brings current match awareness into every lesson. He is also completing a Bachelor of Physical Activity for Health at the Australian College of Physical Education (ACPE), supporting an informed approach to movement, training and healthy participation in sport.

Coaching approach

A complete path from technique to competition.

Each stage supports the next, so improvement carries from the practice court into real points and matches.

01

Assess

Start with a free game analysis to identify movement, technical and tactical priorities.

02

Develop

Build efficient technique, footwork and repeatable patterns with specific feedback.

03

Apply

Use point play to develop decision-making, court positioning and shot selection under pressure.

04

Compete

Prepare routines, tactics and confidence for tournaments, competitions and match goals.
