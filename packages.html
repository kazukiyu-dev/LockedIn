Questions

Before you book.

Is court hire included?

Yes. Court hire is included in the listed Panania rates and in the extended-travel starting prices unless stated otherwise in your final quote.

Are group prices per player?

No. Every amount shown is the total price for the entire group.

Is the first lesson free?

The lesson uses the normal single-lesson or package price. Your first lesson includes a free game analysis to identify your strengths and development priorities.

How is travel pricing confirmed?

Send your preferred suburb and court. Louis will confirm travel time, court availability and the final price before the booking.
